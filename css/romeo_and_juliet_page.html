Characters

Romeo

Romeo is described as a handsome, intelligent, and sensitive boy of around sixteen years of age who is caught in a family feud he is seen as likeable and despite seeming silly and childish he knows alot about his life and the struggles between the families.

Juliet

Juliet Capulet is a young girl who is seen as decisive, passionate and headstrong She is considered a stong female character and doesnt take alot of nonces from people which strengthens her character.

Shown by the whole story and how love can be a huge cause of violence

Theme 2- Family Influence

This theme shows how family can have a huge part of peoples live and even sometimes force there children to do things that they do not wish to do.

Theme 3- Inevitability of Fate

Finally fate is explored and how as much as people try to cahnge what they are fated to do in the end it always comes. This is also expplored by how it can affect someone overall

Context

At the time it was the norm for girls as young as 13/14 to be married and have children. Men were considered adults early on in life. Shakespeare broke the norms by creating Juliet as a strong female character
